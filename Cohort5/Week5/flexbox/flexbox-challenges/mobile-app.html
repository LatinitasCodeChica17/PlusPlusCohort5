Latinitas offers certification programs for students allowing them to learn the basics of real-world skills such as website design, coding, journalism, and content creation. If you have a student that is interested in media and/or technology, Latinitas' certification programs for teens are the perfect way to complement their education and allow them to learn new skills!

The Code Chica cohort is a 8-week long certification program for students to learn the in's and out's of JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, with opportunities to hear from real world professionals about their career path. This program is completely virtual and free. The age range for this program is 13 years old - 18 years old.
