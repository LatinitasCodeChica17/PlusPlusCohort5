Code Chica offers a free coding program designed for girls aged 14-18 to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Participants are organized into small cohort groups of around 10 students, meeting every Saturday for 8 weeks. Throughout the program, they collaborate with instructors and guest speakers who bring expertise in computer science, engineering, and related fields. Upon completing the course, participants not only gain valuable experience but also receive a cash stipend as a rewarding outcome.
